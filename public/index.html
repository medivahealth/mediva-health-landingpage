Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Mediva Health (“Mediva,” “we,” “us”) respects your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website, waitlist, and future services.

Information we collect

We may collect information you provide directly—such as name, email, phone number, and messages submitted through our forms—as well as basic technical data (e.g. device type, browser, and approximate region) needed to operate and secure our services.

How we use information

  • To respond to inquiries and manage beta or early-access sign-ups
  • To improve our products, safety, and user experience
  • To comply with law and protect the rights and safety of users and others

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with service providers who assist our operations under strict confidentiality, or when required by law.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your choices

Where applicable, you may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at info@mediva-health.com.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of our services after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Safety Protocols

How Mediva approaches safe, responsible guidance

Not a substitute for emergency care

Mediva is not an emergency service. If you believe you or someone else is experiencing a medical emergency, call your local emergency number immediately.

Informational layer

Our tools are designed to support understanding and triage-style information, not to replace diagnosis or treatment by a licensed clinician who knows your full history.

Clinical oversight

We structure our systems around medically reviewed content and ongoing quality checks. Automated outputs may still be incomplete or incorrect; always follow advice from your healthcare provider when it differs from app content.

Data handling

Health-related information you share with us is handled with heightened care. See our Privacy Policy for details on collection, use, and your rights.

Reporting concerns

If you notice unsafe, misleading, or harmful behavior in our services, please report it to info@mediva-health.com so we can investigate.

FAQ

Questions & Clarity

Is Mediva a replacement for my doctor? ↓
Mediva acts as a triage and informational layer. While we provide clinically-verified guidance, we are designed to work alongside your primary care physician, not replace them for critical emergencies or physical procedures.
How secure is my medical data? ↓
End-to-end encryption for your most sensitive health data. Mediva is fully HIPAA and GDPR compliant, ensuring that your records remain strictly confidential and under your control.
How is the AI trained? ↓
Our neural network is trained exclusively on peer-reviewed medical journals and validated clinical data. We have a dedicated Clinical Review Board of licensed doctors who continuously audit model performance.
Is Mediva really free? Are there hidden fees? ↓
Core guidance and conversational triage are free for users during our rollout. We may introduce optional premium features in the future, but we will always clearly disclose pricing before you opt in—no surprise charges.
What should I do in a medical emergency? ↓
Mediva is not for emergencies. If you believe you or someone else is in immediate danger, call your local emergency number right away (e.g. 112 in the India). Use Mediva only for non-urgent questions and general health information.
Where is Mediva available? ↓
We are expanding region by region to comply with local healthcare and privacy regulations. Join the beta to be notified when Mediva launches in your country, and to get early access when your region goes live.
